Client Overview

Who is the client?
The client is a small business owner.

What is the product or service?
The clients business is selling cacti.

Who is the target audience?
The target audience are people looking for a house plant that's different from the average plant that you'd find in the typical plant nursery. Aimed at city dwellers, the other type of customer the website is aimed at are people who would like a plant but have limited time to take care of one.
