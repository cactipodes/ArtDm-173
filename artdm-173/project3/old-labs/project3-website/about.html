Who We Are

We love cacti, and we want you to love cacti too. We are a small company that provides cacti to city dwellers all over the globe because we believe people should have the plant of their dreams in their home. We are passionate about the plants that are native to our region, and we want to share our knowledge with you.

Based in Arizona, we source our cacti from their native home, the Americas. We work with local growers to help promote healthy ecosystems and local economic opportunities. Illegal harvesting of cacti and other succulents can have devastating ecological effects on the ecosystems they come from. We are committed to protecting the plants and ecosystems that we love and by supporting us you help too. If you have any questions about our plants or if you have any concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.
