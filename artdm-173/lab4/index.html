The Cozy Homestead

We're a Bed and Breakfast off the beaten path, where weary travelers come to recharge thier spirits

If you are planning a stopover in South County, you don't want to miss out on this charming spot!

Set on 3 acres of beautiful woodland, The Cozy Homestead Bed and Breakfast is a remodled hunting cabin that dates back over a century. With careful renovations over the years and thoughtful preservation of the land, The Cozy Homestead has been given new life and boasts modern touches that compliment the home’s original history. If you are looking for a hotel quality accommodations, but looking for a more personal touch then please don’t hesitate to contact us. We are available for family vacations, weddings, or a rustic romantic getaway. We are ideally located for a wide range of down to earth outdoor activities. This charming outdoor destination, boasts bikable and walkable trails around the property. If you're just looking to relax, the front and back deck offer a great place to hang out and observe nature. While you're here we also provide our guests with breakfast and dinner daily, aswell as daily locally sourced personalised cocktails and wine selection. Rooms are well serviced and stocked, each room featuring a private bathroom and high quaility organic bedding. We hope our home offers such a luxury quality of life it makes you feel like home away from home.
